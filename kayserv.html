THE SERVER

"THE SERVER" is an HP ProLiant DL380 Gen9 that was destined for the dumpster at a company.

  • I was in the right place at the right time and saved it from said dumpster.
  • It is currently hosting about 4TB of backups, system images, and Linux ISOs. It has 10TB of storage, with a mass SSD upgrade in the works.
  • The server is running in RAID5 with a dead storage cache battery.
  • I have installed VMWare ESXi 7 on it and it is configured to be almost completely headless.
  • I have used it to teach myself the basics of server grade hardware and software, including setting up Active Directory, a VPN, NAS, Virtual machines, and networking (Cisco switches on the way!).
  • I have also played around with Ubuntu Server, and am currently teaching myself how to deploy a LAMP stack.

To top it all off, this server sits on a block of plywood in a college apartment, with 2 drives missing because I couldn't afford spares when I got it.
It is the epitome of jankiness, and I guard it with my life. I also have a Dell SAN from 2009.
